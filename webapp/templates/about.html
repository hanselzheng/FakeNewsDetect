About Us


Scammer Scanner is a web-based application designed to identify fake news articles based on user input. Users are able to input news articles, and the system employs machine learning to classify whether the article is reliable or unreliable.



How the Process Works


1. News Dataset for Model Training
Collection of news articles where each article is labeled as either “real” or “fake”.

2. Preprocess Text

  • Remove Non-Alphabetic Characters (punctuations, numbers, commas, quotes, etc.)
  • Convert to Lowercase
  • Remove Stop Words (Examples: a, an, the, and, it, for, but, in, my, your)
  • Stemming: Process of reducing words to their root or base form.


3. TF-IDF Vectorizer (Term Frequency - Inverse Document Frequency)
Scoring method that highlights words that might contain useful information in a given document.
Term Frequency (TF) : the frequency of certain words and phrases in a document.
Inverse Document Frequency (IDF) : the rarity of the word across multiple documents.

4. Logistic Regression Algorithm
Machine learning algorithm used for binary classification tasks. It models the probability of an input belonging to one of two classes, real or fake.

5. The Trained Model is used to Predict Articles from Users
Once the Logistic Regression model is trained on the labeled dataset, it can be used to predict whether a user-submitted article is real or fake.
